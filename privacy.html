Privacy Policy

Last updated · March 2026

1. Introduction

20n ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website ("Site").

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Automatically collected · standard server logs may record your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used solely for maintaining Site security and performance.
  • Voluntarily provided · if you contact us via email, we collect the information you choose to share (name, email address, message content).

3. What We Don't Collect

We do not use cookies for tracking. We do not use third-party analytics services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Use of Information

Any information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Maintain and improve the Site
  • Ensure Site security

5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any data we hold. Server logs are retained for a limited period and automatically deleted. Email correspondence is stored securely and only accessed by authorized team members.

6. Third-Party Services

The Site uses Google Fonts, which may collect standard web request data (IP address, browser type) as described in Google's Privacy Policy. No other third-party services are used.

7. Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable French law, you have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • File a complaint with the CNIL (French data protection authority)

8. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at contact@20n.dev.
